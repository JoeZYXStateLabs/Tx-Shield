Industry Applications

Where Tx Shield powers institutional privacy and compliance.

Payment Layer

Combines Multi-Party Computation (MPC) with Web2 payment networks to enhance key management, risk modeling, and transaction privacy. Tx-Shield enables distributed authorization, privacy-preserving AML analytics, and fraud detection without exposing user data.

Chain Integration

Deployable across L1 and L2 environments with NxN-party MPC architecture. Tx-Shield ensures private yet auditable transactions, enabling scalable and compliant privacy layers for stablecoins, RWA, and institutional DeFi ecosystems.

Darkpool Settlement

Off-chain matching and MPC-based clearing layer where only counterparties can view trades while regulators gain permissioned visibility. Tx-Shield brings confidentiality, liquidity, and auditability together for institutional settlement.

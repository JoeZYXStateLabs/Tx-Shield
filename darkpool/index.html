Darkpool Settlement

Built on Tx-Shield’s primitives—private auditable transactions, metadata protection, and FAST MPC—the darkpool settlement workflow enables confidential order handling and policy-controlled transparency for institutional trading and treasury operations.

Confidential Order & Quote Handling

  • Orders and quotes are encrypted; only direct counterparties observe trade details while the public ledger remains blind to sensitive fields.
  • Protects liquidity strategies and counterparty identities against information leakage.

MPC-Based Clearing & Settlement

  • Threshold-authorized clearing with distributed key control; settlement proofs preserve confidentiality while supporting auditor access.
  • Designed for high-volume transfer-class operations aligned with blockchain settlement rails.

Regulatory Auditability by Design

  • Selective disclosure to designated regulators or auditors via multi-key-shard policies.
  • Enables compliance reviews without broad data exposure to venues or non-participants.

Metadata-Safe Analytics

  • Aggregated, policy-bounded analytics over encrypted trade histories and participant identifiers.
  • Risk and market-surveillance signals without revealing raw identity or position data.

Summary

Tx-Shield provides the cryptographic foundation for institutional darkpools that require both confidentiality and regulatory confidence, bringing privacy, liquidity, and auditability into a unified settlement workflow.
